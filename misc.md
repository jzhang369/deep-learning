Anaconda报错：Conda SSL Error: OpenSSL appears to be unavailable on this machine. OpenSSL is required to download and install packages.

在Anaconda>Library>bin目录下找到

libcrypto-1_1-x64.dll
libssl-1_1-x64.dll

将以上两个文件放入

Anaconda>DLLs，重启shell
